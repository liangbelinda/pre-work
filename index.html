Who is Belinda Liang?

Hello, my name is Belinda Liang. I graduated from Hunter College in May 2020 with a B.A. in Media Studies and a minor in Computer Science. I am a very hands-on person, I love to build things and take things apart. I find that I am best at expressing myself through creative art.

I became interested in UX Design after taking an Interactive Installations class, in which I learned to transform spaces into interactive experiences. This class really opened my eyes to designing for user experience. I realized that every aspect of design from visual to sound can have an impact on the user, so I am interested in designing experiences that are fun and engaging for the user.

Some of my hobbies include:

  • Hiking
  • Biking
  • Playing Video Games and Board Games
  • Learning new skills (Currently learning Blender and skateboarding)

I am an aspiring UX Designer. I hope to make a positive impact on my community through design. I will bring my creativity, curiosity, and willingness to learn new skills.
